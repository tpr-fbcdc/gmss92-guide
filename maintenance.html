Annual Pre-Season Checklist

Complete these tasks before each heating season to prevent common flash codes and ensure reliable operation.

1. Condensate & Drainage System

Prevents 2-Flash and 3-Flash codes

  • Flush the Condensate Trap
    Remove the white plastic trap and flush with warm water and white vinegar to clear biological growth and mineral deposits.
  • Prime the System
    Pour 1 cup of water into the uncapped vertical vent pipe to ensure the trap is primed and not air-locked.
  • Verify Pitch
    Check the vestibule panel with a torpedo level. Ensure ¼" per foot slope toward the drain side.
  • Inspect PVC Runs
    Check horizontal exhaust runs for "bellies" or sags that could trap water and increase back-pressure.

2. Ignition & Burner Maintenance

Prevents 1-Flash codes

  • Clean the Flame Sensor
    Remove the flame sensor (Part #0130F00010) and gently clean the metal rod with a light abrasive pad (Scotch-Brite) to remove oxidation.
  • Inspect the Igniter
    Check the Hot Surface Igniter for white "hot spots" or visible cracks. Replace if showing signs of thinning or wear.
  • Clear Vacuum Port
    Use a straightened paperclip to clear the plastic nipple on the inducer motor housing.

3. Electrical & Airflow

Prevents 4-Flash and logic errors

  • Replace Air Filter
    Install a fresh 16×25×1 filter. Mark the installation date on the filter frame.
  • The "Pro-Pinch"
    Check for firm tension on spade connectors at the pressure switches and control board headers. Tighten with needle-nose pliers if loose.
  • Clean Blower Compartment
    Vacuum dust and debris from the blower motor area to keep motor and control board cool.
  • Inspect Vent Terminals
    Check intake and exhaust terminations outside for bird nests, leaves, or debris blockage.

Maintenance Frequency Reference

Frequency Task Benefit
Monthly Inspect Air Filter Prevents overheating (4-Flash)
Annual Flush Condensate Trap Prevents pressure switch issues (2/3-Flash)
Annual Clean Flame Sensor Prevents ignition dropouts (1-Flash)
Bi-Annual Inspect Vent Terminals Ensures clear intake/exhaust
Bi-Annual Clean Blower Wheel Maintains proper airflow
